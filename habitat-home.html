Kala Chitta Park Introduction

Welcome to Kala Chitta Habitat National Park, a pristine sanctuary nestled in the heart of natural splendor. This enchanting haven is a testament to the rich biodiversity and vibrant ecosystems that define the region. Established to conserve and protect the unique flora and fauna of the Kala Chitta mountain range, this national park stands as a symbol of our commitment to environmental preservation.

Geography And Location

The park is strategically located within the Kala Chitta mountain range, providing a breathtaking backdrop of towering peaks, lush valleys, and meandering streams.

At the core of Kala Chitta Habitat National Park is a steadfast commitment to conservation.

The park actively engages in sustainable practices, habitat restoration, and wildlife protection programs. These efforts aim to ensure the longevity of the region's unique ecosystems and safeguard the species that call this sanctuary home.
