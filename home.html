National Parks Of Pakistan

The unique characteristics of the climate and natural landscape of Pakistan make it a wildlife-rich country. It is home to plenty of natural habitats for both migratory birds and local animal species. However, the increasing level of human activities can easily hamper biodiversity and ecosystems in these natural settings.

Organizational of the National Park Service Of Pakistan

As of my last knowledge update in January 2022, there might not be a specific National Park Service in Pakistan similar to the National Park Service in the United States. However, Pakistan does have a system of national parks and protected areas managed by various government agencies, including the Pakistan Environmental Protection Agency (Pak-EPA), provincial wildlife departments, and forest departments. Please note that organizational structures and government agencies can change, and it's advisable to check the latest information for the most accurate details.
