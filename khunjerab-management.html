Management And National Park Location

Khunjerab National Park is managed and protected by the Pakistan Wildlife Department. The management focuses on conservation, preservation of the unique ecosystem, and sustainable tourism. Here are key aspects of the management.

Conservation Policies: Conservation Policies The park is managed with a strong emphasis on conservation, aiming to protect the fragile ecosystems and endangered species. Conservation efforts include monitoring wildlife populations, combating illegal poaching, and implementing measures to mitigate environmental degradation.

Tourism Management: Tourism Management Due to its stunning natural beauty, the park attracts tourists, trekkers, and nature enthusiasts. The management implements policies to ensure sustainable tourism, minimizing the impact on the environment while providing visitors with an opportunity to appreciate the park's natural wonders.

Research and Monitoring: Research and Monitoring Ongoing research initiatives focus on understanding the park's ecology, studying wildlife behavior, and monitoring climate change impacts. This information is crucial for making informed decisions regarding conservation strategies and sustainable development.
